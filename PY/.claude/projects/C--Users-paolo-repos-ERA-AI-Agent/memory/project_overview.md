Building an AI agent (ERA-AI-Agent) for a Moldovan law firm called ERA. Currently in Research & Design phase, moving into Designing.

## MVP Scope (decided via stakeholder interview)
Three priority tasks, chosen because they're the most time-consuming and repetitive:
1. **Client offer creation** — personalized PPT offers; biggest time sink, highly repetitive but manual
2. **Document drafting** — contracts based on templates; needs accuracy and efficiency
3. **Document analysis/summarization** — analyzing large files with better accuracy than ChatGPT

## Key stakeholder insights
- Current AI (ChatGPT) is used but requires heavy verification and sometimes produces weak arguments
- Firm wants a **personalized assistant, not full automation** — lawyers reuse outputs, not blindly accept them
- Biggest bottleneck is offer creation (business side), not legal analysis
- Stakeholders don't need explainability most times, just better results
- Internal templates exist and can be reused

## Success criteria
- 30%+ time reduction on offer creation
- Generated outputs require less manual correction than ChatGPT
- Lawyers can reuse outputs instead of rewriting from scratch

**Why:** Targets the most time-consuming repetitive work, aligned with stakeholder expectations.
**How to apply:** Focus on template-driven, structured generation. Quality > full automation. Always consider that end users are lawyers who will review and edit outputs.
